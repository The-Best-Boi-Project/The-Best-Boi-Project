What is the Bestboi Project?

The bestboi project is a group primarily based out of VRchat and similer VR games, based off the bestboi Vrchat community of old. We offer a welcoming and friendly group on our discord and our public world on vrchat! Everyone is welcome with us. We off an Easy to edit and customize free public model, as well as events and other activities.
